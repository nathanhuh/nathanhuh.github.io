Profile

Nathan Huh is a DevOps Engineer at LG Uplus and graduated in 2024 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from USC.

Nathan Huh was born in Busan, Republic of Korea, and moved to Cheonan, South Korea to attend boarding school. He graduated from Bugil Academy Global Leader Program in 2019 and enrolled at the University of Southern California.

He served two years in the South Korean military during the COVID-19 pandemic and returned to USC in 2022. After graduating from USC, he joined LG Uplus as a DevOps Engineer.
