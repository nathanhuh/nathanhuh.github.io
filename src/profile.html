Profile

Nathan Huh (born August 11, 2000) is a Site Reliability Engineer at LG Uplus, and a class of 2024 graduate for Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at USC.

Nathan Huh was born in Busan, Republic of Korea and moved to Cheonan, South Korea to go to boarding school. He graduated Bugil Academy Global Leader Program in 2019 and enrolled to the University of Southern California.

After graduating from USC, he joined LG Uplus as a Site Reliability Engineer.
